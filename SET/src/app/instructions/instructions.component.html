How to Play

Welcome to the game! Frogbert has trouble forming search queries, can you help them?

  1. Enter the search query creator by clicking on the PLAY button.
  2. Frogbert will ask you to help them form a query for a specific question.
  3. Try your best to form a search query that Frogbert can use.
  4. You'll be given points based on how good your query is!
  5. You can use the points you've won to unlock things for Frogbert to wear!
  6. You may even level up when you get points!
  7. There are some tools to help you form your queries...
  8. Use the HINT and CHECK MY SEARCH buttons to help you form better queries!
  9. Be careful though! As you level up, these buttons will start to take points when used!
  10. They may even dissappear when you're a high enough level!
